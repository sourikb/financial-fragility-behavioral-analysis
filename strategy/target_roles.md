- Location: Seattle, Arlington, Austin, New York
- Base salary range: $159,200 – $236,900 USD annually
- Source: LinkedIn (Paola Mallucci, Marketing Science Leader)

**Basic Qualifications:**
- PhD in economics or equivalent

**Preferred Qualifications:**
- Experience in analytics and applied economics
- Experience developing analytic vision for business problems
- Experience building statistical models in R, Python, STATA
- Experience in industry, consulting, government, or academic research
- Experience managing and measuring marketing performance
- Experience presenting to senior leadership
- Knowledge of modern ML methods and their integration with 
  econometric approaches

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Design and build rigorous measurement framework for brand and 
  upper-funnel marketing investment

  awareness on customer preferences, acquisition costs, and revenue
- Apply causal inference methods suited to long-horizon, slow-moving 
  brand effects
- Translate findings into actionable investment recommendations
- Collaborate with data scientists and engineers to operationalize 
  models
