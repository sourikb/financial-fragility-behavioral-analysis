# Content Log

A record of all public content shared on LinkedIn, Substack, and 
other platforms throughout the journey from academic behavioral 
economist to Senior Applied Economist.

This log serves three purposes:
1. Accountability — ensures consistent presence-building
2. Portfolio asset — demonstrates sustained thought leadership 
   over multiple years
3. Strategic alignment — tracks which content drew engagement from 
   which audiences

## Posting Strategy by Phase

### Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Quiet Foundation
- LinkedIn profile updated and connected to 20-30 applied economists 
  at target firms
- Thoughtful engagement with 1-2 posts per week
- No original posts yet

### Phase 2 (Months 7-12): First Public Voice
- One monthly LinkedIn post drawn from capstone project insights
- Optional Substack launch at end of Year 1

### Phase 3 (Months 13-30): Sustained Professional Presence
- Two to three LinkedIn posts per month
- One to two Substack posts per month
- Direct engagement with target firm employees

### Phase 4 (Months 31-36): Strategic Job Market Visibility
- Weekly LinkedIn posts
- Two Substack posts per month
- Strategic content tied to target roles before applications
